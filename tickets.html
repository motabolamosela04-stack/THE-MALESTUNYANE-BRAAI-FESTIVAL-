Ticket Information

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival offers a variety of ticket options designed to suit different visitors attending the event. Each ticket provides access to an exciting experience filled with entertainment, food, and cultural activities.
Visitors are encouraged to purchase their tickets early to avoid disappointment and secure their place at the festival.
