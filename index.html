Welcome To The Maletsunyane Braai Festival

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival is one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho, attracting visitors from different parts of the country and beyond. The event combines food, music, and cultural experiences in a vibrant outdoor setting. It is hosted in Semonkong, a place well known for its breathtaking landscapes and the famous Maletsunyane Falls. Visitors attend the festival to enjoy a unique blend of adventure and Basotho culture.

This festival plays an important role in promoting tourism and supporting local communities. It provides opportunities for local businesses such as food vendors, artists, and performers to showcase their work. Tourists are able to experience authentic Basotho traditions while enjoying modern entertainment. As a result, the event contributes to economic growth and cultural preservation in Lesotho.

Event Details

  • Date: 27-29 November 2026
  • Location: Semonkong, Maseru Lesotho
  • Main Attraction: Maletsunyane Falls
